daInsNam R Documentation

Liability Insurance Coverage for Hunters and Anglers in Mississippi: Variable Definitions

Description

This data set contains variable defintions for the a survey result about liability insurance purchase decision by hunters and anglers in Mississippi, as documented in daIns. It contains 14 observations and 2 columns.

Variable 14 variable abbrevations
Definition Detailed definitions for the 14 variables

Usage

data(daInsNam)

Format

A data frame with 2 columns and 14 rows.

Details

The data set contains the definitions of 14 variables collected from a telephone survey as detailed in daIns

Source

Sun, C., S. Pokharel, W.D. Jones, S.C. Grado, and D.L. Grebner. 2007. Extent of recreational incidents and determinants of liability insurance coverage for hunters and anglers in Mississippi. Southern Journal of Applied Forestry 31(3):151-158.
