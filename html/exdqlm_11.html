nino34 R Documentation

Monthly Niño 3.4 Index.

Description

Monthly Niño 3.4 sea surface temperature (SST) Index time series.

Usage

nino34

Format

A time series of length 936.

Source

https://psl.noaa.gov/gcos_wgsp/Timeseries/Nino34/
