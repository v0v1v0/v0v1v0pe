data18 R Documentation

data18: Ramalho et al. (2005): page 115

Description

Repetition of experiments in block design

Usage

data(data18)

Format

A data frame with 60 observations on the following 4 variables.

treatments

a numeric vector

experiments

a numeric vector

blocks

a numeric vector

response

a numeric vector

References

RAMALHO, M. A. P.; FERREIRA, D. F. and OLIVEIRA, A. C. Experimentacao em Genetica e Melhoramento de Plantas. Editora UFLA, 2005, 322p.

Examples

data(data18)
summary(data18)
