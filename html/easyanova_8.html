data14 R Documentation

data14: Sampaio (2009): page173

Description

Incomplete block design in animals

Usage

data(data14)

Format

A data frame with 28 observations on the following 4 variables.

treatment

a factor with levels A B C D E F G

animal

a factor with levels A1 A2 A3 A4 A5 A6 A7

period

a factor with levels P1 P2 P3 P4

response

a numeric vector

References

SAMPAIO, I. B. M. Estatistica aplicada a experimentacao animal. 3nd Edition. Belo Horizonte: Editora FEPMVZ, Fundacao de Ensino e Pesquisa em Medicina Veterinaria e Zootecnia, 2010. 264p.

Examples

data(data14)
summary(data14)
