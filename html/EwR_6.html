REcoData_Panel

Description

Panel data between 1996-2017 for G8 countries

Usage

REcoData_Panel

Format

A data frame containing :

ULKE Countries

POPG Population Growth

INF Inflation Rate

UR Unemployment Rate

GDP Gross Domestic Product

EXP Export

FDI Foreign Direct Investment

Examples
